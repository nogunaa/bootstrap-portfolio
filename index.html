About

As a frontend developer, my goal is not just to succeed but to thrive in this dynamic and innovative industry. I am driven to break stereotypes and demonstrate that women can excel and make significant contributions in the world of technology. I believe that diversity and inclusion are crucial for progress, and I am eager to be a part of driving positive change in the tech community.
Every day, I am inspired by my daughter, who looks up to me with admiration and hope. I am determined to be a role model for her and show her that women are capable of achieving anything they set their minds to, even excelling in fields traditionally perceived as male-dominated. That's why I have decided to pursue a career in software development.
My journey into software development is not just about my own growth; it is also about paving the way for future generations of women in technology. I want my daughter and other young girls to see that they can aspire to anything they desire and pursue their passions fearlessly.
